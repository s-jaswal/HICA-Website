Our Mission

The Hindu Indian Cultural Association fosters community, preserves heritage, and promotes cultural understanding through vibrant events and engagement.

The Hindu Indian Cultural Association (HICA) is dedicated to serving as a vibrant hub for individuals of Hindu and Indian descent, as well as anyone interested in experiencing the rich tapestry of Indian culture.

Community
Building a strong, inclusive community for all.
Heritage
Preserving and celebrating rich Indian traditions.
Understanding
Promoting cultural appreciation and connection.
