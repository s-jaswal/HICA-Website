Our Mission

The Hindu Indian Cultural Association (HICA) is dedicated to serving as a vibrant hub for individuals of Hindu and Indian descent, as well as anyone interested in experiencing the rich tapestry of Indian culture. Our core mission revolves around three pillars: building a strong, inclusive community, actively preserving and celebrating the diverse heritage of India, and promoting a deeper understanding and appreciation of Indian traditions among a wider audience.
